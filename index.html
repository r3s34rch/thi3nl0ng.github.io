About Me

I am pursuing my PhD in Computer Science here at NTNU. My research topic is data mining. In somehow, others might say it is related (less) to machine learning (ML), no false positive, of course. In my works, I build automatically tuning models (optimize cost), specificcally for change detection. I have interest in Optimization, and anything related to security. I spent some time on ML, mostly with Capsule Net. I was really excited in CapNet and unsupervised learning, found (thought) some ideas, but I still need to finish other stuffs with my PhD first. So, I will be back to it later, sure soon. I know/can understand some basic Math, that at least I can count how much money in my pocket.
I am a person of realism, like playing with number, e.g. lottery. My biggest dream is to crack money exchange chain.
If you are reading this, you might do searching something or curious on my things. If you find anything interesting or get what you want, please drop me an echo, because I haven't found it for a very long time.
